Chow Mein

Description

A Cantonese style Chow mein dish. One of my favorite noodle dish. Chow mein stands for stir fried noodle.

Ingredients: The Sauce

  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp Shaoxing wine/oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Ingredients: The Stir Fry

  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 ounces sliced yellow onion
  • 4 scallions
  • 3 ounce Bok choy
  • 3 ounces bean sprouts
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sauce: Combine all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Cut the onions, bok choy (or any vegetable you like), stringy ends of the bean sprouts
  4. Heat wok and stir fry the onions and vegetables. Take it out of the wok and put in a bowl
  5. Stir fry the noodle in the wok
  6. Add the vegetable and sauce into the wok
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
