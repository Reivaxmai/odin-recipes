Huzarensalade

Discription

A classic Potato Salad dish in Suriname.

Ingredients

  • 2 kgs Potatoes
  • 7 tbsp White Sugar
  • 250 g Mayonnaise
  • 13 oz Corn Kernels
  • 6 oz Pickles/Red Beets
  • 6 oz Silver Onions

Steps

  1. Peel, cut and boil the Potatoes
  2. Mash Potatoes and mix in all the ingredients
  3. Cool it and enjoy
