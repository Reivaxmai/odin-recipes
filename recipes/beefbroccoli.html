Beef with Broccoli

Description

This is a beef stir fry with some broccoli. A takeout classic.

Main Ingredients

  • 340 g beef
  • 454 g broccoli
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 small piece ginger
  • 1 stem scallions

Marinade Ingredients

  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cooking wine

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp cooking wine
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
